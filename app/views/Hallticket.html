INSTRUCTIONS TO THE CANDIDATE :
1. Hall Ticket issued to you is an important document. Please preserve it carefully till the admissions.
2. Candidate shall arrive at the examination hall at least 1 hour before commencement of the examination. This will enable the candidate to familiarize himself/herself with OMR response sheet.
3. Candidates will not be allowed to enter the examination hall after 11:00 AM, once the examination has commenced.
4. Candidates are directed to carry the following to the examination hall (a). Good ball point pen (black), (b). Writing pad (c). HB Pencils (d). an Eraser (e). Sharpener.
5. Candidate should not bring log books, tables, calculators, tabs, cell phones, communicable/ programmable electronic gadgets etc. into examination hall.
6. Candidates must remain seated in their allotted seats till the completion of examination. In no case they will be allowed to leave the examination hall till 1:30 PM. Before leaving, the candidates must return OMR response sheet to the invigilator.
7. Candidates are advised to go through the instructions given for marking answers and other entries on the OMR response sheet thoroughly.
8. POLYCET- Entrance Test is conducted for candidates who desires to take up admissions into (a) Diploma level programmes offered by SBTET in Government, Government-Aided, Private Un-Aided Polytechnics in Telangana State. (b) Diploma Courses offered by Professor Jayashankar Telangana State Agricultural University (PJTSAU) both University and affiliated polytechnics in the Telangana State. (c) Diploma Course offered by PVNRTVU, Hyderabad. (d) Tentatively the 6 Years integrated (2+4) undergraduate courses offered by Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies, Basara (RGUKT) (e) Diploma courses offered by Sri. Konda Lakshman Telangana State Horticulture University (SKLTSHU)
9. Two separate Ranks will be allotted for the candidate who appeared for POLYCET: (a) MPC Rank: will be issued to students who desires to take admission in to Diploma Courses in Polytechnics affiliated to SBTET and 6 Years integrated under graduate courses offered by RGUKT based on marks obtained in Maths, Physics and Chemistry. (b) MBiPC Rank: will be issued to the students who desires to take admission in to courses offered by PJTSAU, SKLTSHU and PVNRTVU Diploma Courses based on marks obtained in Maths, Physics, Chemistry and Biology.
10. Candidates can opt to take admission in to either SBTET Diploma Courses offered in Polytechnics (or) Courses offered by PJTSAU/ PVNRTVU/ SKLTSHU /6 Years integrated (2+4) undergraduate courses offered by RGUKT (Tentatively).
11. No separate application form for admission is prescribed for the candidate desirous of seeking admission in to Engineering (or) NonEngineering Diploma courses offered by SBTET in Polytechnics, the filled-in application for POLYCET itself will be treated as application for admission in to courses offered in Polytechnics.
12. Separate admission notification will be issued for admission into various Diploma Courses offered by PJTSAU on the University website http://www.pjtsau.edu.in & PVNRTVU on its website www.tsvu.nic.in & RGUKT on its website https://www.rgukt.ac.in and SKLTSHU on its website http://skltshu.ac.in.
13. The OMR response sheet should be handled carefully and the candidates are advised not to fold or wrinkle or tear the OMR sheet under any circumstances. Candidates are advised not to scribe or make any marks on the response sheet except marking answers other relevant data at appropriate places. Candidates shall note that they will not be given under any circumstances a second OMR sheet.
14. Candidate shall mark the correct answer in the OMR response sheet by shading in dark the appropriate circle with HB pencil. They should not use ball pen for this purpose under any circumstances.
15. Candidates should not suffer from any of the following defects: a) Mentally retarded b) Blindness c) Deaf and Dumb d) Chronic Heart, Lung, Kidney, Liver diseases e) Muscular dystrophy and if found the candidate forfeit chance to appear examination
